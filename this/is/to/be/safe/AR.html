Tasks Overview

Steps:
  1. Ensure Timesheet Submission:
    • Confirm that all timesheets are submitted.
    • Clear out any pending job not in the system.
  2. Run Job Report:
    • Generate the job report as per the requirement (specific month, customer, or job).
  3. Check Estimates:
    • Review estimates for each job to ensure that all times align with the calculated estimates.
    • Verify that the amounts are correct for billing.
  4. Create Invoice in QuickBooks:
    • Log into QuickBooks and create an invoice.
    • Ensure the customer and job site are correctly mentioned.
    • Include detailed job information in the description box.
  5. Verification and Sending:
    • Verify the invoice details with Chris.
    • Send the invoice to the customer once verified.

QBO: Creating Invoice from the estimate.

Steps:
  1. Access QBO and open the "Payments" section.
  2. Match payments received against issued invoices.
  3. Ensure all payments are correctly allocated.
  4. Mark invoices as paid in QBO.
Relation:

QBO: Payment tracking and reconciliation.

Steps:
  1. Identify overdue invoices in QBO.
  2. Generate payment reminders for overdue customers.
  3. Send reminders via email or the QBO messaging system.
  4. Follow up with customers if necessary.
